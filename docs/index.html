🔒 Privacy Policy

ProcStep

Last updated: June 2026
The short version: ProcStep stores everything on your device. We don't run any servers, we don't collect analytics, and we don't have user accounts. The only time any data moves at all is when you personally choose to share a procedure with another device — and even then, it goes directly between devices, never through us.

What ProcStep is

ProcStep is a work-procedure management app for manufacturing and operations teams. It lets you create step-by-step procedures with photos, organize them by category, and share them with other devices running ProcStep.

Data we collect

We don't collect any data. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporting service, no advertising ID, and no account system in this app. We have no server, so there is nothing for us to receive, log, or store about you.

Where your data lives

Everything you create in ProcStep — procedures, steps, photos, categories — is stored locally on your device in a private app database. It is never uploaded anywhere by us, because we don't operate any backend to upload it to.

Permissions this app requests, and why

PermissionWhy it's needed
CameraTo scan a QR code shown by another device when receiving a shared procedure over WiFi.
Photos / StorageTo attach reference photos to a step. The app uses the system photo picker, so it only ever accesses the specific photos you choose.
WiFi stateTo find your device's local network address so it can be shown as a QR code for direct device-to-device transfer.
Network state / InternetUsed only to run a short-lived local server on your own WiFi network during a transfer. ProcStep does not contact the public internet or any remote server.

Sharing procedures between devices

ProcStep offers two ways to share a procedure with another device, both initiated entirely by you:

  • WiFi transfer — your device and the receiving device connect directly over your local network. The data never leaves that network and never passes through any server we control.
  • Share via other apps — ProcStep can export a procedure as a file, which you then send using an app of your choice (e.g. WhatsApp, email, Telegram). Once it leaves ProcStep, that app's own privacy policy applies to the transfer.

Children's privacy

ProcStep is intended for workplace/industrial use and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children, in part because we do not collect data from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds optional cloud sync — we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above before that feature ships.
